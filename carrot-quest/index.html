Carrot Quest is a third person puzzle game in which we control Jack J. White, a rabbit whose goal is to move through a series of spatial reasoning, head breaking puzzles and reach the carrot at the end of each level. His movement is limited to a 2-space jump, having to use its environment and obstacles to his advantage.
